Help & Support

Find answers, manage bookings, and reach our team — all in one place.

Account & Profile

How to update profile information?
Go to Accounts & Settings from the side menu. Tap Edit Profile to update your name, city, phone, or date of birth. Changes are saved automatically.
Forgotten your password?
On the login screen, tap Forgot Password. Enter your registered email and we'll send you a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
Changing email notifications.
Head to Accounts & Settings → Notifications. Toggle on or off the categories you want (event reminders, offers, weekly digest). Changes take effect immediately.

Bookings & Tickets

How to view my purchased tickets?
Open the side menu and tap Your Orders. All confirmed bookings appear here with QR codes. You can also download tickets as PDF for offline access.
Process for canceling a booking?
Go to Your Orders, select the booking, and tap Cancel Booking. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days depending on the event's cancellation policy.
Troubleshooting a booking issue.
If payment was deducted but no confirmation arrived, wait 10 minutes and refresh. If it still hasn't appeared, submit a support ticket below with your payment reference number.

Rewards & Payments

How do points work?
You earn 1 MeetPoint for every ₹10 spent. Points can be redeemed at checkout for discounts. Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.
Can I use multiple rewards?
You can combine MeetPoints with one promo code per transaction. Bank cashback offers and spin-wheel discounts are applied automatically at checkout and cannot be stacked with each other.
Accepted payment methods.
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking, and MeetMap Gift Cards. All transactions are secured with 256-bit encryption.
